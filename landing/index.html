Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Creeator

How do AI workflows work?

AI workflows are customizable sequences of automated steps that handle your content creation process. You can chain together research, writing, editing, SEO optimization, and publishing steps. Each workflow is powered by specialized AI agents that understand your brand voice and requirements.

Can I customize the AI agents for my brand?

Absolutely! You can train AI agents with your brand guidelines, tone of voice, style preferences, and existing content examples. The agents learn your brand's unique voice and maintain consistency across all generated content.

What integrations do you support?

We integrate with popular CMS platforms (WordPress, Webflow), social media (Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook), email marketing tools (Mailchimp, ConvertKit), and many more. You can also export content in various formats or use our API for custom integrations.

How accurate is the AI-generated content?

Our AI models are trained on high-quality datasets and fine-tuned for content creation. While the content is typically 90%+ ready to publish, we always recommend human review for accuracy, brand alignment, and final polish. Most users find they only need minor edits.

Can I collaborate with my team?

Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans include team collaboration features like shared workspaces, approval workflows, role-based permissions, comments, and real-time editing. You can assign tasks, track progress, and maintain quality control.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a free Starter plan with 3 AI agents and 10 workflows per month. Professional and Enterprise plans come with a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.
